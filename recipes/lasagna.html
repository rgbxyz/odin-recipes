Lasagna

Mmm mmm yummy

This deliciously strange dish can be created by stuffing all manner of ingredients into a slow cooker and waiting for magic to happen

Ingredients

  • Turmeric 2 bootfuls
  • JazzHands 3 cups
  • Stinky cheese 1 bunch

Steps

  1. Find a big Bowl
  2. Fill with Turmeric and hold your breath
  3. shake those JazzHands furiously
  4. dump in that stanky cheese and step back to admire
