Chicken Parmesan

Tasty delights and breaded night sweats

It may not look like much but it sure does taste like a dream come true

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup marinara sauce
  • 1 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 cup cheese

Steps

  1. Find a big Bowl
  2. Fill with Turmeric and hold your breath
  3. shake those JazzHands furiously
  4. dump in that stanky cheese and step back to admire
